ABOUT

Hello there! I'm a second-year BSIT student named Marchel Ngujo. I fell in love to Cordova, Cebu, I knew I had to share its spells across the world. This blog is my dedication to Cordova, presenting its breathtaking beaches, historical treasures, and vibrant society as seen through my eyes. Come along on a journey with me as I discover this island paradise's best-kept secrets!

a little background

Cordova isn't just another island escape. It's a beautiful place with beautiful beaches, tall bridges, and reminders of the past produced within. Take a dive into waters that are a witness amazing structures such as the Cebu-Cordova Link Expressway, and walk in the footsteps of Lapu-Lapu, the warrior who faced off against Magellan. Go outside your comfort zone and discover Cordova!
