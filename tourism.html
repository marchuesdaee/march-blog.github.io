A Magical Escape to Cordova's Glowing Blooms

10,000ROSES

TOURISM

A field of Glowing Roses

The 10,000 roses in Cordova, Philippines, aren't real flowers, but thousands of glowing LED lights shaped like roses! It's actually a cafe called "10,000 Roses Cafe & More." The roses light up at night, making it a magical place to visit for photos, sunsets, and enjoying the view of Cordova and Cebu City. It's pretty popular with tourists and locals! So, basically, it's not a field of real roses, but a cafe with a super pretty light display. Hope that helps!

Imagine an ocean filled with ten thousand artificial, LED-lit roses. They create a magical dusk over the cafe, setting the stage for pictures that will have everyone who follows wanting. Thanks to this rose wonderland's incredible visual impact, friends snap pictures of each other surrounded by flowers, couples pose among the flowery arches, and single visitors enjoy the imaginative environment.
