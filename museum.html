Connecting with Cordova's History Through its Museum

MUSEUM

HISTORY

The museum's origins date back to 2007, when Cordova wanted to share its own identity with the world and maintain its history. The community joined together to build a place where stories could be spoken, artifacts could be shown, and traditions could be cherished because it realized how important its past was. With echoes of the past echoing through its walls, the Cordova Museum was formed.
