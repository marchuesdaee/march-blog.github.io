Welcome to Parola! The origins of Parola can be traced to the flourishing marine trade of Cebu during the late 1800s. Spanish authorities started building a nautical beacon in 1896 after realizing how important it was. Constructed with strong concrete and brick, the lighthouse towered over the sea, its light acting as a watchful guide for ships navigating through dangerous hazards and choppy waters.

Parola is more than just a tourist destination, it's a location to get escape from the grind, get back in touch with nature, and make lifelong memories.

Visit the lively eateries in Parola to taste delicious Filipino specialties and fresh seafood meals. As you eat, take in the gorgeous sunset and make memories that will last a lifetime.

You may get a personal look at the beauty of nature by traveling to Parola. It also serves as a call to action. Reduce your waste, practice responsible tourism, and support local environmental efforts. Recall that each of us is a guardian of this priceless ecosystem.
